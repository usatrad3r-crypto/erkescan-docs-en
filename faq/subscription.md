# Subscription & Billing

### What are the subscription plans?

ErkeScan offers three plans with the same full feature set — the difference is duration and savings. Monthly: $200/month. Quarterly: $540 for 3 months (10% off). Annual: $1,680 for 12 months (30% off).

### What payment methods are accepted?

ErkeScan accepts cryptocurrency payments through CryptoCloud. You can pay with BTC, ETH, USDT, and other major cryptocurrencies.

### How quickly does my subscription activate?

Your subscription activates automatically within 5–10 minutes after your crypto payment is confirmed on-chain.

### Can I subscribe through the Telegram bot?

Yes. Open [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot), tap **Subscribe**, choose your plan, and complete the payment. Works exactly the same as subscribing through the web app.

### Where can I check my subscription status?

In the web app: go to the **Billing** page. In Telegram: tap **Profile** in [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot). Both show your current plan and expiration date.

### What happens when my subscription expires?

You'll lose access to premium features: custom alert delivery stops and signal scanner access is restricted. Your account and alert configurations are preserved — when you resubscribe, everything is restored.

### Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of the service and cryptocurrency payment, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact [@erkescan\_support](https://t.me/erkescan_support) on Telegram to discuss.

### Do you send renewal reminders?

Yes, you'll receive a notification before your subscription expires so you can renew without interruption.

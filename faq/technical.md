# Technical Support

### Which devices and browsers are supported?

ErkeScan works on any device with a modern web browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone. We recommend Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. The Telegram bot works on all platforms where Telegram is available.

### Can I use ErkeScan on multiple devices?

Yes. Log in with the same account on any number of devices. Your alerts, favorites, and settings sync across all sessions.

### The screener isn't loading or updating. What should I do?

Try these steps: (1) Refresh the page. (2) Clear your browser cache. (3) Check your internet connection. (4) Try a different browser. If the issue persists, contact support — there may be a temporary service disruption.

### Telegram notifications aren't coming through. What should I check?

Make sure you: (1) Started the bot by sending `/start` to [@erkescan\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_bot). (2) Have an active subscription. (3) Have at least one alert configured in the web app. (4) Haven't blocked or muted the bot in Telegram. (5) Have Telegram notifications enabled on your device.

### Discord is connected but access isn't working.

Make sure you are connecting the Discord account associated with your community membership. Try disconnecting and reconnecting via the Integrations page. If the issue persists, contact support.

### Browser-specific settings storage

The screener saves your preferences (column arrangement, sort order, favorites) in your browser's local storage. If you switch browsers or clear your data, you may need to reconfigure these preferences.

### I found a bug or have a feature request.

Contact us via the Support option in the Telegram bot or reach out to [@erkescan\_support](https://t.me/erkescan_support). We actively develop the platform and appreciate user feedback.

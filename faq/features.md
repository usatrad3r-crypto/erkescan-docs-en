# Features & Tools

### What is AutoTrade Pro?

AutoTrade Pro is an automated signal service included with every subscription. Signals are delivered to a private VIP Telegram channel and include: coin, direction (LONG or SELL), entry price, stop-loss, three take-profit targets (TP1/TP2/TP3), signal confidence level, and a potential loss/profit ratio. Access is granted automatically after your subscription is confirmed via [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot).

### How many custom alerts can I create?

Unlimited. There is no cap on the number of alerts you can set up.

### What metrics can I use for custom alerts?

Over 30 metrics including: price change (5m/15m/1h/8h/1d), open interest change, volume, relative volume, volume delta, volatility, ticks, funding rate, and market cap — each across multiple timeframes.

### How does the Signals tab work?

The Signals tab in the web app evaluates coins across multiple momentum factors and assigns a grade from A (strongest) to F (weakest). Signals that score exceptionally high get the HC (High Confidence) badge. Use signals as a starting point for your own analysis.

### What are Spaghetti Charts?

A multi-line visualization that overlays 4–10 coins on one chart for direct performance comparison. Useful for spotting sector rotation, relative strength, and divergence between correlated assets.

### What chart types are available?

15 types: Price, Cumulative Return, Return by Day/Hour, Volume (24h and intraday), CVD Dollar, Open Interest, OI Changes & Price Changes, OI Snapshots, Funding Rate, Annualized Basis, Liquidations, Sector Performance, and Spaghetti Charts.

### How often is the screener data updated?

The screener streams data in real time via Server-Sent Events (SSE). Updates appear on your screen within seconds of occurring on the exchange.

### What is Relative Volume and why does it matter?

Relative Volume compares current volume to the 24-hour average. A value of 1.0 is normal. Values above 2.0 mean double the normal activity — which often precedes a breakout or major price move. It's one of the best early warning indicators.

# General Questions

### What is ErkeScan?

ErkeScan is a cryptocurrency futures market intelligence platform. It collects real-time data from Binance, Bybit, and Hyperliquid, processes it through advanced analytics, and delivers actionable insights through a web dashboard, Telegram bot, and Discord community. It includes a real-time screener, signal scanner, custom alerts, interactive charts, and whale tracking.

### How is ErkeScan different from other crypto screeners?

Three key differences: (1) ErkeScan aggregates data from three exchanges, not just one, giving you a more complete view. (2) Our Signal Scanner uses a proprietary 9-factor grading system that evaluates the full picture, not just single metrics. (3) You get unlimited custom alerts — most platforms either limit alerts or charge extra for them.

### Which exchanges does ErkeScan support?

Binance Futures, Bybit (linear perpetuals), and Hyperliquid (decentralized perps). We track all major futures pairs across these three exchanges.

### Do I need trading experience to use ErkeScan?

Basic understanding of crypto futures trading is helpful, but ErkeScan is designed to be intuitive. The screener is essentially a sortable table, alerts are simple "if X happens, notify me" rules, and our documentation explains every metric. If you're new, start with the [Getting Started](../getting-started/create-your-account.md) guide and the [Glossary](../glossary.md).

### Does ErkeScan execute trades for me?

No. ErkeScan is a market intelligence and alert tool — it shows you data, detects opportunities, and notifies you. All trading decisions and order execution are yours to make on your preferred exchange.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. ErkeScan uses Clerk for authentication, which provides enterprise-grade security. We do not have access to your exchange accounts or trading credentials. ErkeScan only reads public market data — it never connects to your exchange wallet or trading account.

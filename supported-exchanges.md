# Supported Exchanges

ErkeScan aggregates real-time futures data from three major cryptocurrency exchanges. Each exchange provides different data streams that feed into our screener, signals, and analytics.

## Binance Futures

**The world's largest crypto derivatives exchange by volume.**

Binance Futures is ErkeScan's primary data source. We track all USDT-margined perpetual futures contracts, including:

- Real-time price and mark price
- Trade-by-trade volume with buy/sell classification
- Open interest (aggregate and per-symbol)
- Funding rates (every 8 hours)
- Liquidation events
- 24-hour ticker data
- Spot prices (for basis calculation)

Binance provides the deepest liquidity and the widest selection of trading pairs, making it the reference exchange for most metrics.

## Bybit

**A leading derivatives exchange known for linear perpetual contracts.**

ErkeScan tracks Bybit's linear perpetual futures, providing:

- Real-time price data
- Volume and trade flow
- Open interest tracking
- Funding rate data

Bybit data helps ErkeScan identify cross-exchange divergences — when Bybit's OI or volume moves differently from Binance, it can signal exchange-specific positioning that may resolve into price action.

## Hyperliquid

**A decentralized perpetual exchange.**

Hyperliquid represents the growing DeFi derivatives space. ErkeScan tracks:

- Perpetual futures prices
- Volume data
- Open interest
- Funding rates

Including Hyperliquid allows ErkeScan users to see how decentralized and centralized markets compare — divergences between DEX and CEX positioning often provide unique trading signals.

## Cross-Exchange Benefits

By aggregating data from all three exchanges, ErkeScan provides:

- **More complete open interest data** — total OI across all venues, not just one exchange
- **Volume aggregation** — see total market activity, not just one platform
- **Divergence detection** — spot when one exchange's data differs from others
- **Better signal accuracy** — signals based on multi-exchange data are more reliable than single-exchange analysis

## Coverage

ErkeScan tracks all major cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.) plus an extensive list of altcoins — covering virtually every futures pair available on the supported exchanges.

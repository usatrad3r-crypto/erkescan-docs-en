# Discord

ErkeScan integrates with Discord to provide community access and role-based features for subscribers.

## Connecting Your Discord

1. Log in to [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com)
2. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
3. Click **Connect Discord**
4. Authorize the connection through Discord's OAuth flow
5. Your account is now linked

## Trader Role

Active subscribers automatically receive the **Trader** role in the ErkeScan Discord server. This role gives you:

- Access to subscriber-only channels
- Community discussion with other active traders
- Shared insights and market analysis from the community

Your Trader role syncs with your subscription status. When your subscription is active, the role stays active. If your subscription expires, the role is removed.

## How Sync Works

ErkeScan periodically verifies that Discord members with the Trader role have active subscriptions. This keeps the community authentic — only paying subscribers have access to VIP channels.

## Troubleshooting

**Role not appearing?** It may take a few minutes for the role to sync after connecting. Make sure you're a member of the ErkeScan Discord server.

**Wrong Discord account?** Disconnect via the Integrations page and reconnect with the correct account.

**Role disappeared?** Check that your subscription is still active in the Billing section of the web app.

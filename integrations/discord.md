# Discord

ErkeScan integrates with Discord to provide access for members of our closed community.

## Connecting Your Discord

1. Log in to [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com)
2. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
3. Click **Connect Discord**
4. Authorize the connection through Discord's OAuth flow
5. Your account is now linked

## Community Access

Discord access to ErkeScan is available exclusively to members of our closed community. If you are part of the community, connect your Discord account to activate your access to the platform.

## Troubleshooting

**Access not activating?** Make sure you are connecting the Discord account associated with your community membership. Sync may take a few minutes after connecting.

**Wrong Discord account?** Disconnect via the Integrations page and reconnect with the correct account.

**Still having issues?** Contact support on Telegram: [@erkescan\_support](https://t.me/erkescan_support)

# Telegram Bot

The ErkeScan Telegram Bot ([@erkescan\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_bot)) is your direct line to real-time alerts, signals, and account management — all from your phone or desktop Telegram app.

## Getting Started

1. Open [@erkescan\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_bot) in Telegram
2. Tap **Start** or send `/start`
3. Choose your language (English or Russian)
4. You'll see the main menu with all available options

## Main Menu

| Option | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Subscribe** | View subscription plans and purchase directly in Telegram |
| **View Features** | Browse screenshots and examples of ErkeScan's tools in action |
| **FAQ** | Get answers to the 10 most common questions |
| **Profile** | Check your current subscription status and expiration date |
| **Support** | Contact the support team |

## What the Bot Sends You

Once subscribed and connected, the bot delivers:

**Custom Alert Notifications** — When any coin meets the conditions you set in the web app, you'll receive an instant message with the coin name, metric value, and current price.

**AutoTrade Pro Signals** — Pre-configured signals generated by ErkeScan's analysis engine, including:

- Coin and direction (LONG or SHORT)
- Suggested entry price
- Stop-loss level
- Three take-profit targets (TP1, TP2, TP3) in a pyramid structure
- Risk/reward ratio
- Signal confidence score

## Subscribing via Bot

You can purchase a subscription directly in Telegram:

1. Tap **Subscribe** in the main menu
2. Choose your plan (1 month, 3 months, or 1 year)
3. Complete the cryptocurrency payment via the provided link
4. Your subscription activates automatically within 5–10 minutes

## Language Support

The bot supports **English** and **Russian**. You can change your language at any time through the bot menu.

## VIP Telegram Chat

Active subscribers get access to the **VIP Telegram group** — a private chat with other ErkeScan users where traders share setups, discuss signals, and exchange market insights.

## Rate Limiting

The bot has a 3-second cooldown between actions to ensure stable performance. If you tap too quickly, just wait a moment and try again.

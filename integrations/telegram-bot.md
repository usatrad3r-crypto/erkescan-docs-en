# Telegram Bots

ErkeScan uses two separate Telegram bots, each with a distinct purpose.

## [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot) — Subscriptions & Account

This is your account management bot. Use it to:

- Subscribe to a plan and make payments
- View available plans and features
- Check your subscription status and expiration date
- Access FAQ
- Contact support

**Getting started:**

1. Open [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot)
2. Tap **Start** or send `/start`
3. Choose your language (English or Russian)
4. Use the main menu to subscribe or manage your account

**Subscribing via bot:**

1. Tap **Subscribe** in the main menu
2. Choose your plan (1 month, 3 months, or 1 year)
3. Complete the cryptocurrency payment via the provided link
4. Your subscription activates automatically within 5–10 minutes

## [@erkescanalert\_bot](https://t.me/erkescanalert_bot) — Custom Alert Notifications

This bot delivers your custom alert notifications. When a coin meets the conditions you set in the web app, you receive an instant message with:

- Coin name and symbol
- The triggered metric and its value
- Current price

**To receive alerts:**

1. Open [@erkescanalert\_bot](https://t.me/erkescanalert_bot) and send `/start`
2. Make sure you have an active subscription
3. Create at least one alert in the web app at [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com)
4. Alerts will be delivered to this bot automatically

## Language Support

Both bots support **English** and **Russian**. You can change your language at any time through the bot menu.

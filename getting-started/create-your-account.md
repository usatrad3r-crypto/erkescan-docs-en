# Create Your Account

Getting started with ErkeScan takes just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Visit the Web App

Go to [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.

## Step 2: Register

Create your account using your email address and a password, or sign up with a supported OAuth provider (Google, etc.). You'll receive a verification email — click the link to confirm your account.

## Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll land on the main dashboard. From here you can:

- Open the **Screener** to see live market data
- Browse the **Signal Scanner** for current momentum setups
- Check out **Charts** to analyze any coin in depth
- Visit **Alerts** to start creating your own custom rules

## Step 4: Subscribe

To unlock the full platform — including custom alerts, Telegram notifications, signal scanner access, and VIP community — you'll need an active subscription. Head to the **Pricing** page to choose a plan.

See [Choose a Subscription Plan](choose-a-plan.md) for details on pricing and payment.

## Step 5: Connect Your Integrations

After subscribing, connect your Telegram and Discord accounts to receive alerts and access the VIP community.

See [Connect Telegram & Discord](connect-integrations.md) for setup instructions.

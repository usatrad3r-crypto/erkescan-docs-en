# Connect Telegram & Discord

To get the most out of ErkeScan, connect your Telegram and Discord accounts. This enables real-time alert delivery and community access.

## Connect Telegram

Telegram is where you'll receive all your custom alert notifications and signal updates.

1. Open [@erkescanalert\_bot](https://t.me/erkescanalert_bot) in Telegram and send `/start` — this bot delivers your custom alert notifications
2. Open [@erkescan\_premium\_bot](https://t.me/erkescan_premium_bot) and send `/start` — this bot handles subscriptions and account management
3. Make sure you have an active subscription and at least one alert configured in the web app

## Connect Discord

Discord access is available exclusively to members of our closed community.

1. Log in to [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com)
2. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
3. Click **Connect Discord**
4. Authorize ErkeScan to access your Discord account
5. Your membership is verified and access is activated

## Troubleshooting

**Telegram bot not responding?** Try sending `/start` again or restart the conversation with the bot.

**Discord access not activating?** Make sure you are connecting the Discord account associated with your community membership. Sync may take a few minutes.

**Need help?** Contact support at [@erkescan\_support](https://t.me/erkescan_support) on Telegram.

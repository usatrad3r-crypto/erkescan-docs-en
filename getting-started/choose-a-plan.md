# Choose a Subscription Plan

ErkeScan offers three subscription plans. All plans include the same full feature set — the only difference is duration and savings.

## Plans

| Plan | Duration | Price | Savings |
|------|----------|-------|---------|
| **Monthly** | 1 month | $200 | — |
| **Quarterly** | 3 months | $540 | 10% off |
| **Annual** | 1 year | $1,680 | 30% off |

## What's Included (All Plans)

- Full access to the Real-Time Screener with all 30+ metrics
- Signal Scanner with momentum grading (A–F) and HC signals
- Unlimited custom alert creation
- Telegram alert delivery
- 16 types of interactive charts and analytics
- Whale tracking and trade monitoring
- VIP Telegram community access
- Discord integration for closed community members
- Multi-device access (use on desktop, tablet, phone)

## How to Pay

ErkeScan accepts cryptocurrency payments through CryptoCloud. Supported currencies include BTC, ETH, USDT, and other major cryptocurrencies.

To subscribe:

1. Log in to [app.erkescan.com](https://app.erkescan.com)
2. Go to the **Pricing** page
3. Select your preferred plan
4. Complete the payment using your crypto wallet
5. Your subscription activates automatically within 5–10 minutes after payment confirmation

You can also subscribe directly through the [Telegram bot](../integrations/telegram-bot.md) by tapping **Subscribe** in the main menu.

## Managing Your Subscription

Visit the **Billing** page in your account to:

- View your current subscription status and expiration date
- See payment history
- Renew or upgrade your plan
